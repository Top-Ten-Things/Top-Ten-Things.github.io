2021 PULLEY EXPERIMENT

In this project, I was trying to answer the question of how the design of a simple machine will change its mechanical advantage. I used pulleys from VEX Robotics, a candle (load), and dental floss to design and build 3 variations of my machine. The results showed that my hypothesis (as the number of pulleys increase, the mechanical advantage will increase) was correct.
