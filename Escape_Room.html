Homemade Haunted House
2020

COVID-19 had just hit, so we couldn't do anything for Halloween like trick or treat or go to a hounted house. So my dad, mybrother, and I decided to build a haunted house in out guest room for my mom. I used VEX robotics to create scary things like a book that opened and closed its "mouth," a hockey mask that had a red glow, and a falling axe that stopped right above my mom's head. We also had fake clowns, zombies, scary music, and blood lying around. At the end, my brother was waiting in his alien costume to scare my mom. Overall, this haunted house was a great way to express my creativity and engineering skills.
