Pop The Lock

After coming from the arcade, my brother challenged me to code Pop The Lock so that he could practice in his free time and win the jackpot. Since almost all the part are circluar or involve circular motion, this was an extremely hard project. I tried many methods of making the red line spin, but all of them failed. A couple days later, I read about parametric equations and realized this would the perfect solution to my problem. Using a given value of t (a variable), I can find the x and y coordinates of the 2 points on the line. Plus, if I want to speed it up, all I have to do is make t increase by a greater amount. After figuring that out, it was smooth sailing. This was one of my hardest projects.
